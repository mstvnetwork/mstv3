Custom YouTube Player Demonstration

These YouTube players are designed to autoplay (muted), have no seek bar, and are unresponsive to direct clicks/touches on the video area. Use the "Unmute/Mute" button to control audio.

**Note:** Due to browser policies, videos will start muted. Click "Unmute" to hear sound.
